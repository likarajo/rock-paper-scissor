How to play:

User needs to click on one of the buttons named ROCK, PAPER, or SCISSOR.

The computer's choice is randomized.

Based on the choice of both the user and the computer, winner is decided based on the below rules:

ROCK overpowers SCISSOR; SCISSOR overpowers PAPER; PAPER overpowers ROCK
